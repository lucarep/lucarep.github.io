Intro⌗

In this new update to the simulator, has been added what represents probably, the most important stochastic process, that is, the Brownian motion. Calculated in this way: $$P(t) = P(t-1) + \text{Random step}(t), \text{for t }=1,…,n$$ Where the Random Step(t) is: $$\sigma \cdot \sqrt{\frac{1}{n}} \cdot Z(t)$$ Where Z(t) is a N(0,1) random variable.
